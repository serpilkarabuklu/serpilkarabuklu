 I earned my bachelor's degree from the Foreign Language Education Department at Boğaziçi University where I was trained for pedagogical methods for different levels. I completed my practicum in a private school in Istanbul. Then, I was also trained for teaching linguistics at the graduate level pedagogy class at  Purdue University. 

## Courses taught as Instructor of Record

* _Introduction to Linguistics_. Purdue University. Fall 2019, Spring 2018.

## Guest Lectures

I have had the opportunity to talk about the structures of sign languages as a guest lecturer in various courses. 

* Karabüklü, S. Strategies to Express Time in a Tenseless Language: Turkish Sign Language (TİD), _ELL 417: Language Across Modalities_, Istanbul Bilgi University, Istanbul, Turkey. December 2022.
* Barutçu, C. &amp; Karabüklü, S. Deaf Community and TİD in Turkey, _LING 260: American Deaf Community: Language, Culture, and Society_, University of Chicago, Chicago, IL, USA. November 2022
* Karabüklü, S. Annotation Tools in Sign Language Linguistics, _ASL 361: The Structure of ASL I: Phonology and Morphology_, Purdue University, West Lafayette, IN, USA. September 2021.
